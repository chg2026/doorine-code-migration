One important thing I need to flag before we phase this
I just looked under the hood and found the two apps don't speak the same auth language right now:

apps/crm uses Supabase auth directly (Supabase session in the browser, JWT bearer to the server)
apps/chg-rehab uses iron-session cookies (a separate, older session system)
So "same login lands in CHG Platform" isn't just a copy/paste of the login page — we have to make chg-rehab actually accept Supabase logins. That's solvable but it's the biggest single piece of work in this whole project, and it needs to be Phase 1 because everything else depends on it.
